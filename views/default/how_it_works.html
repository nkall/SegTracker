How It Works

  1. Create a Strava account. This site will pull all of your information directly from Strava. No need to register with us.

  2. Record your athletic activity using one of the many compatible GPS devices as you normally would while using Strava.

  3. Upload your activity from the device to Strava.

  4. Use our website to analyze and compare your data against yourself. View graphs of your performances on rides or segments to better improve yourself.
